About ProFix

ProFix is a locally owned hardware repair business based in North Providence, Rhode Island. We provide professional repair services for smartphones, tablets, and laptops, serving customers throughout Providence and surrounding areas.

Our technicians specialize in screen replacements, battery repairs, charging port repairs, and general hardware diagnostics. We focus on fast turnaround, transparent pricing, and reliable service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality device repair accessible and stress-free. We aim to help our customers extend the life of their devices while receiving honest service and clear communication every step of the way.

Local Service You Can Trust

ProFix proudly serves North Providence and the greater Rhode Island area. Customers can visit our physical location or contact us for repair estimates, service updates, and support.
