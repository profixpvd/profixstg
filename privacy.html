Privacy Policy

ProFix values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and device details when you submit a repair quote request or contact us.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to provide repair quotes, service updates, appointment confirmations, and customer support.

SMS Communications

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from ProFix, your phone number will be used only for transactional or marketing messages based on your consent selection. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP.

Data Sharing

ProFix does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
